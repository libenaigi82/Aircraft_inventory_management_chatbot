Step 5 — warehouses.js

Unlike the previous files, this one is operational data.

Since no airline publicly shares warehouse locations and codes, we'll use realistic educational warehouses and clearly document them as simulated.

This is completely acceptable for academic projects.

Why these warehouses?

We'll assume our AI system is deployed for an airline with two MRO hubs.

Main Hub
BLR-MRO

Bengaluru is one of India's largest aviation and aerospace hubs.

Secondary Hub
DEL-MRO

Delhi is another major aviation maintenance location.

This gives us a realistic multi-warehouse inventory system.
